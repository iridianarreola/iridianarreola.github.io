FAQs

What are lash extensions?

Lash Extensions are a cosmetic enhancement attaching synthetic or natural fibers to the natural eyelashes.

Will lash extensions damage my natural lashes?

No, when applied properly by a professional , with perfect isolation and weight eyelash extensions will not harm your natural lashes.

What should I do before my lash appointment?

  • Thoroughly was your eyes with oil free face wash or eye makeup remover.
  • Do not wear any eye makeup.
  • Do not curl your lashes!
